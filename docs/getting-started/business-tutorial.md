# Business Getting Started Guide

**Welcome, Business Owner!** This guide will help you set up your business profile and start hiring daily workers in Bali's hospitality sector.

**Estimated time: 10-15 minutes**

---

## 📋 Overview: Your Hiring Journey

```
Create Account → Complete Business Profile → Post Your First Job → Review Applications → Hire Workers → Track Attendance → Pay via Wallet
```

This guide covers all 7 steps to get you from registration to paying your first hired worker.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### 📱 On Mobile (Android)

1. Download **Daily Worker Hub** from Google Play Store
2. Open the app and tap **"Register"**
3. Select **"Business"** as your account type
4. Enter your phone number (+62 format: e.g., +6281234567890)
5. Enter the 6-digit verification code sent via SMS
6. Set a secure password for your account

### 💻 On Web Dashboard

1. Go to [dailyworkerhub.com](https://dailyworkerhub.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### ✅ Account Created!

Your account is now ready. You'll be redirected to complete your business profile setup.

---

## Step 2: Complete Business Profile Setup

Complete your profile in **3 simple steps**. Your progress is automatically saved if you need to pause.

### Step 2.1: Business Identity

**What you'll need:**
- Business name (e.g., "Villa Sunset Bali")
- Business type (select from dropdown)
- Phone number (Indonesian format: +62xxxxxxxxxxx)
- Email address

**Business types available:**
- Hotel / Villa
- Restaurant
- Event Venue
- Spa / Wellness
- Other

**Tips:**
- Use your official business name for trust and verification
- Phone number must be Indonesian format (+62) for SMS notifications
- This information is visible to workers searching for jobs

### Step 2.2: Business Location

**What you'll need:**
- Business address (street address in Bali)
- Location coordinates (auto-detected or manual pin on map)

**Tips:**
- Accurate location helps nearby workers find your jobs
- Workers can filter jobs by distance from their location
- Your full address is only shared with workers after they apply

### Step 2.3: Description & Review

**What you'll need:**
- Business description (optional but recommended)
- Website URL (optional)
- Accept Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

**Review your profile:**
- Double-check all information is correct
- A complete profile builds worker trust
- Workers are more likely to apply to well-presented businesses

**Click "Complete Setup"** to finalize your business profile.

## Step 3: Top Up Your Wallet

Before posting jobs, you'll need funds in your wallet to pay workers.

### 💳 Wallet Top-Up Methods

1. **QRIS** — Scan any QRIS-enabled payment app (GoPay, OVO, Dana, Bank apps)
2. **Virtual Account** — Transfer from any Indonesian bank
3. **GoPay Direct** — Instant transfer via GoPay

### 💰 How to Top Up

1. Go to **Wallet** section in your app/dashboard
2. Tap **"Top Up"**
3. Select your preferred payment method
4. Enter the amount (minimum Rp 50,000 recommended for first jobs)
5. Complete the payment
6. Funds appear instantly in your wallet

**Tip:** Keep your wallet funded to avoid delays when hiring workers.

---

## Step 4: Post Your First Job

### 📝 Creating a Job Posting

1. Go to **"Jobs"** section
2. Tap **"Create New Job"** (+ button)
3. Fill in the job details:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Job Title** | Brief, clear job name | "Daily Cleaner Needed" |
| **Category** | Type of work | Housekeeping, Kitchen, Driver, etc. |
| **Description** | Detailed work requirements | "Clean 10 villa rooms, provide supplies" |
| **Requirements** | Skills/experience needed | "Experience with villa cleaning preferred" |
| **Date & Time** | When work starts and ends | "March 28, 2026, 08:00 - 16:00" |
| **Budget Range** | Daily wage range | "Rp 150,000 - Rp 200,000" |
| **Workers Needed** | Number of workers | "2 workers" |
| **Location** | Work site address | Pre-filled from your business address |
| **Deadline** | Application close date | "March 27, 2026, 18:00" |

4. Mark as **"Urgent"** if you need workers immediately
5. Review and tap **"Post Job"**

### ⏰ Job Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Open** | Job is visible to workers, accepting applications |
| **Filled** | All worker slots have been assigned |
| **In Progress** | Workers have checked in, job is active |
| **Completed** | Job finished, workers marked as completed |
| **Cancelled** | Job was cancelled by you |

## Step 5: Review Applications

### 📋 Viewing Applications

1. Go to **"My Jobs"**
2. Select your posted job
3. Tap **"View Applications"**

### 👤 Application Information

For each applicant, you'll see:
- **Worker name and photo**
- **Rating** (stars and number of reviews)
- **Skills badges** (verified skills)
- **Experience level** (Junior, Mid, Senior)
- **Location** (distance from your business)
- **Applied time** (when they applied)

### ⭐ Worker Verification Badges

Workers may have these trust badges:
- **Face Verified** — Identity confirmed via selfie
- **Address Verified** — Home address confirmed
- **Skilled** — Skills tested and verified
- **Top Rated** — Consistently high ratings (4.5+)

### 💬 Communication

- **Chat** — Message workers before hiring
- **Voice Call** — Call workers directly
- Discuss job details, confirm availability, answer questions

### ✅ Accepting Applications

1. Review the applicant's profile and ratings
2. Optionally chat to ask questions
3. Tap **"Accept"** to hire them
4. Worker receives notification and job assignment

---

## Step 6: Track Attendance

### 📱 Check-In/Check-Out

Workers must check in and out to record work hours:

**Check-In:**
- Worker taps **"Check In"** button at job location
- GPS location is recorded automatically
- You receive notification when worker checks in

**Check-Out:**
- Worker taps **"Check Out"** when work is done
- Total hours worked is calculated
- You receive notification when worker checks out

### ✅ Confirming Attendance

After worker checks out:
1. Go to the job in **"My Jobs"**
2. View the worker's attendance record
3. Confirm hours worked are correct
4. Optionally rate the worker (1-5 stars)

**Tip:** Accurate attendance records help with:
- Fair payment calculations
- Building worker trust
- Resolving disputes

---

## Step 7: Pay via Wallet

### 💸 Payment Process

1. Go to **"My Jobs"** and select completed job
2. View **"Workers Assigned"**
3. Confirm hours worked match check-in/check-out
4. Tap **"Pay"** — payment goes directly from your wallet to worker's wallet

### 📊 Payment Summary

After payment:
- Worker receives funds in their wallet instantly
- Transaction recorded in both wallets
- 2-way rating system prompted (both parties rate each other)
- Receipt/transaction history saved

### 💡 Payment Tips

- **Top up regularly** — Keep your wallet funded
- **Pay promptly** — Workers appreciate fast payment
- **Rate workers** — Helps build trust in the community
- **Dispute resolution** — Contact support if issues arise

## 🔧 Common Tasks

### Editing a Job
1. Go to **"My Jobs"**
2. Select the job
3. Tap **"Edit"**

1. Go to **"My Jobs"**
2. Select the job
3. Tap **"Cancel"**

1. Go to completed/cancelled job
2. Tap **"Repost"**
3. Update details if needed
4. Post again

### Viewing Worker Ratings
1. Go to **"Workers"** section
2. Select a worker
3. View their rating, reviews, and completed jobs

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "My job has no applications"
- Check if the job deadline hasn't passed
- Ensure the wage is competitive for that category
- Mark job as **Urgent** to increase visibility
- Try reposting with adjusted description

### "Worker didn't check in"
- Contact the worker via chat or phone
- If no response, you may reassign the slot to another worker
- Report persistent issues to support

### "Payment failed"
- Check your wallet balance is sufficient
- Try a different top-up method
- Contact support with transaction ID

### "Worker is unresponsive"
- Use the in-app chat or call feature
- If unreachable before job start, reassign the slot
- Rate the worker to help other businesses

### "Need to update my business profile"
- Go to **"Settings"** → **"Business Profile"**
- Edit the information you need to change
- Changes apply to future job postings
