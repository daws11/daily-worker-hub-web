# Worker Getting Started Guide

**Welcome, Worker!** This guide will help you set up your profile and start finding daily work opportunities in Bali's hospitality sector.

**Estimated time: 10-15 minutes**

---

## 📋 Overview: Your Job Search Journey

```
Create Account → Complete Profile → Browse Jobs → Apply → Check In → Work → Get Paid
```

This guide covers all 8 steps to get you from registration to earning your first paycheck.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### 📱 On Mobile (Android)

1. Download **Daily Worker Hub** from Google Play Store
2. Open the app and tap **"Register"**
3. Select **"Worker"** as your account type
4. Enter your phone number (+62 format: e.g., +6281234567890)
5. Enter the 6-digit verification code sent via SMS
6. Set a secure password for your account

### 💻 On Web Dashboard

1. Go to [dailyworkerhub.com](https://dailyworkerhub.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. Select **"Worker"** as your account type
4. Enter your email address and create a password
5. Check your email for a verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

### ✅ Account Created!

Your account is now ready. You'll be redirected to complete your profile setup.

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile Setup

Complete your profile in **3 simple steps**. Your progress is automatically saved if you need to pause.

### Step 2.1: Personal Information

**What you'll need:**
- Full name (as shown on your ID)
- Phone number (Indonesian format: +62xxxxxxxxxxx)
- Date of birth

**Tips:**
- Use your real name — businesses need to verify who is arriving for work
- Phone number must be Indonesian format (+62) for SMS notifications
- A complete profile builds trust with businesses

### Step 2.2: Your Location

**What you'll need:**
- Home address (street address in Bali)
- Location coordinates (auto-detected or manual pin on map)

**Tips:**
- Accurate location helps businesses find nearby workers
- Jobs can be filtered by distance from your location
- Your full address is only shared with businesses after you accept a job

### Step 2.3: Skills & Experience

**What you'll need:**
- Primary skill category (select from available options)
- Experience level (Junior, Mid, Senior)
- Short bio (optional but recommended)

**Available skill categories:**
- Housekeeping / Cleaning
- Kitchen / Cook
- Bartender / Waiter
- Driver
- Event Staff
- Security
- Spa / Massage
- Gardener
- General Labor
- Other

**Experience levels:**
- **Junior** — 0-1 years experience
- **Mid** — 1-3 years experience
- **Senior** — 3+ years experience

**Tips:**
- Choose the category that best matches your skills
- Accurate experience level helps businesses set fair expectations
- A good bio increases your chances of getting hired

**Accept Terms of Service** and tap **"Complete Setup"**.

## Step 3: Browse Available Jobs

### 🔍 Job Marketplace

After setup, go to the **"Jobs"** section to see all available daily work opportunities.

### 📊 Understanding Job Cards

Each job listing shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Brief, clear job name | "Villa Room Cleaner" |
| **Business** | Name of the hiring business |
| **Category** | Type of work | Housekeeping, Kitchen, etc. |
| **Date & Time** | When work starts and ends | "March 28, 2026, 08:00 - 16:00" |
| **Wage** | Daily payment amount | "Rp 150,000" |
| **Location** | Distance from your location | "3.2 km away" |
| **Rating** | Business rating from workers | "4.8 stars" |

### 🔎 Search & Filter Jobs

**Search bar:**
- Enter keywords like "cleaner", "kitchen", "driver"
- Results update automatically

**Filters available:**
- **Category** — Filter by type of work
- **Distance** — Jobs within X km of your location
- **Date** — Jobs available on specific dates
- **Wage range** — Minimum daily wage
- **Experience level** — Required experience
- **Verification badges** — Only show verified businesses

**Sort options:**
- **Newest first** — Most recently posted
- **Nearest first** — Closest to your location
- **Highest wage** — Best paying first
- **Best rated** — Top-rated businesses

### 💡 Finding the Right Job

**Tips for finding good jobs:**
- Filter by your primary skill category
- Set distance filter to cover reachable areas
- Check business ratings before applying
- Apply early — popular jobs fill up fast
- Look for **Verified Business** badge for trusted employers

---

## Step 4: Apply for Jobs

### 📋 Applying to a Job

1. Tap on any job card to see full details
2. Review the job description, requirements, and wage
3. If interested, tap **"Apply"**
4. Confirm your application

**What happens after you apply:**
- Business receives your application with your profile
- Business reviews your rating, experience, and badges
- Business may chat with you to ask questions
- You'll be notified if you're accepted or declined

### 📱 Application Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Application submitted, waiting for review |
| **Accepted** | Business hired you — report to the job |
| **Declined** | Business did not select you — try other jobs |
| **Cancelled** | Application was cancelled |

### 💬 Communicating with Businesses

After applying, businesses may contact you:
- **In-app chat** — Message workers directly
- **Voice call** — Call workers to confirm details

**Tip:** Respond quickly to business messages to increase your chances of being hired.

### ⭐ Building Your Reputation

**Verification badges you can earn:**
- **Face Verified** — Identity confirmed via selfie
- **Address Verified** — Home address confirmed
- **Skilled** — Skills tested and verified
- **Top Rated** — Consistently high ratings (4.5+)

**Tips for getting better ratings:**
- Arrive on time for work
- Complete all assigned tasks
- Communicate professionally
- Ask questions if unclear

---

## Step 5: Check In to Your Job

### ✅ Once You're Accepted

When a business accepts your application:
1. You'll receive a notification
2. Go to **"My Bookings"** in the Jobs section
3. View your accepted job with all details

### 📱 Check-In Process

On the day of work:

**Check-In:**
1. Go to **"My Bookings"**
2. Find your upcoming job
3. When you arrive at the location, tap **"Check In"**
4. Your GPS location is recorded automatically
5. Business receives notification that you've arrived

**Requirements for check-in:**
- You must be within a reasonable distance of the job location
- You can only check in during the scheduled time window
- Your check-in time is recorded for attendance

**Tips:**
- Check in only when you have actually arrived at the workplace
- Be on time — late check-in may affect your rating
- If GPS fails, use the QR code scanner as backup

---

## Step 6: Do Your Work & Check Out

### 💼 During the Job

- Complete the tasks assigned by the business
- Communicate any issues immediately via in-app chat
- Follow workplace safety and hygiene guidelines
- Be professional and courteous

### 📱 Check-Out Process

When your work is complete:

**Check-Out:**
1. Go to **"My Bookings"**
2. Find your active job
3. When finished, tap **"Check Out"**
4. Your GPS location and total hours are recorded
5. Business receives notification

**After check-out:**
- Business confirms your hours worked
- Business processes your payment
- Both parties are prompted to rate each other

---

## Step 7: Get Paid via Wallet

### 💰 Payment Process

After work is completed and confirmed:

1. Business approves your hours worked
2. Payment is processed from business wallet to your wallet
3. You receive funds in your **Earnings Wallet** instantly

### 📊 Viewing Your Earnings

**Wallet Dashboard shows:**
- **Available Balance** — Funds you can withdraw
- **Pending Balance** — Funds waiting to be processed
- **Transaction History** — All past earnings

**Payment summary:**
- Wage amount earned
- Business name
- Date and time worked
- Transaction ID for reference

### 📈 Tracking Your Earnings

**Earnings section shows:**
- Total earned this month
- Breakdown by job category
- Comparison to previous months
- Upcoming scheduled payments

---

## Step 8: Withdraw Your Earnings

### 🏧 Withdrawal Options

**Bank Transfer:**
- Withdraw to any Indonesian bank account
- Minimum withdrawal: Rp 50,000
- Processing time: 1-3 business days

**E-Wallet:**
- Transfer to GoPay, OVO, Dana, or LinkAja
- Instant transfer (where available)

### 💳 How to Withdraw

1. Go to **Wallet** section
2. Tap **"Withdraw"**
3. Select withdrawal method (Bank or E-Wallet)
4. Enter amount (minimum Rp 50,000)
5. Enter destination account details
6. Confirm and submit

**Tips:**
- Set up withdrawal details in Settings for faster future transfers
- Withdraw regularly to avoid accumulating large balances
- Keep transaction records for your own accounting

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "My application was declined"
- Don't be discouraged — try other available jobs
- Check if your profile and ratings are competitive
- Update your skills or experience level if needed

### "I can't check in"
- Ensure GPS location is enabled
- Make sure you're within range of the job location
- Check that it's within the scheduled check-in time window
- Try using QR code scanner as backup

### "Business didn't approve my hours"
- Contact the business via in-app chat
- Check your check-in and check-out times
- Contact support if unresolved

### "Payment not received"
- Check wallet for pending vs available balance
- Allow 1-3 business days for bank transfers
- Contact support with transaction ID if delayed

### "I was marked absent"
- If you checked in but were marked absent, contact the business
- Provide your check-in/check-out records
- Contact support for dispute resolution

### "Need to update my profile"
- Go to **"Profile"** section
- Edit information as needed
- Changes take effect immediately
