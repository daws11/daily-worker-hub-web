## 🔍 PENYAKIT YANG DISEMBUHKAN (PROBLEM STATEMENT)

### Untuk Bisnis (Pengguna Layanan)

| Masalah | Dampak | Frekuensi |
|---------|--------|----------|
| **Susah cari worker** | Bisnis terganggu, lost revenue | Sering |
| **Kualitas tidak terjamin** | Layanan ke tamu buruk | Sering |
| **Proses manual** | Waste time, tidak efisien | Selalu |
| **Pembayaran tidak jelas** | Konflik dengan worker | Sering |
| **Tidak ada backup** | Kalau worker tidak datang, bingung | Sering |

### Untuk Worker

| Masalah | Dampak | Frekuensi |
|---------|--------|----------|
| **Susah cari pekerjaan** | Pendapatan tidak stabil | Sering |
| **Tidak ada kepastian pembayaran** | Gajian molor | Sering |
| **Tidak ada verifikasi** | Tidak dipercaya bisnis | Selalu |
| **Komunikasi buruk** | Missed jobs, salah paham | Sering |
| **Tidak ada career growth** | Stuck di level yang sama | Selalu |

## 💡 SOLUSI YANG DITAWARKAN

### Untuk Bisnis
- ✅ Akses ke pool worker terverifikasi (KYC)
- ✅ Booking system yang jelas (interview → hire → work → review)
- ✅ Payment integrated (top-up wallet,auto pay)
- ✅ Real-time chat dengan worker
- ✅ Rating & review system
- ✅ Backup worker kalau yang utama tidak bisa

### Untuk Worker
- ✅ Jobs yang tersedia каждый hari
- ✅ Pembayaran terjamin (wallet system)
- ✅ KYC badge = kepercayaan
- ✅ Flexible schedule (atur availability)
- ✅ Builds reputation (badges, ratings)
- ✅ Career path (Classic → Pro → Elite → Champion)

## ⚖️ TWO-SIDED MARKETPLACE DYNAMICS

### Supply & Demand Balance

```
                     DEMAND (Businesses)
                           │
                           ▼
    ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │                                         │
    │      EQUILIBRIUM POINT                  │
    │   (Sustainable marketplace)             │
    │                                         │
    └─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                           ▲
                           │
                     SUPPLY (Workers)
```

**Kenapa ini tricky:**
- Kalau supply > demand → Workers bored, tidak dapat uang
- Kalau demand > supply → Businesses frustrated, tidak dapat worker
- Kalau supply sangat rendah → Businesses pergi ke kompetitor

**Solusi kita:**
- **Geographic concentration** - focus 1 area dulu
- **Batch recruitment** - recruit workers sesuai demand yang sudah ada
- **Incentive alignment** - workers dapat bonuses untuk early booking

## 📊 PRICING STRATEGY

### Philosophy: Penetration Pricing

**Alasan:**
- Marketplace butuh liquidity (supply + demand)
- Too expensive = nobody joins
- Better to grow first, monetize later

### Pricing Evolution

| Phase | Strategy | Rationale |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| **Beta** | 0% commission | Attract initial users |
| **Soft Launch** | 15% commission | Test willingness to pay |
| **Scale** | 15% + subscriptions | Additional revenue streams |

### Commission Structure

| Job Value | 15% Commission | Worker Gets | Business Cost |
|-----------|----------------|-------------|---------------|
| Rp 200,000 | Rp 30,000 | Rp 170,000 | Rp 200,000 |
| Rp 300,000 | Rp 45,000 | Rp 255,000 | Rp 300,000 |
| Rp 500,000 | Rp 75,000 | Rp 425,000 | Rp 500,000 |
| Rp 1,000,000 | Rp 150,000 | Rp 850,000 | Rp 1,000,000 |

## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

### 1. Trust is Everything
- KYC verification builds trust
- Ratings dari both sides build trust
- Wallet escrow builds trust

### 2. Density Beats Scale
- Better to have 20 workers in Seminyak than 5 workers across Bali
- Higher match rate, faster response, word-of-mouth

### 3. Quality Over Quantity
- 10 great workers > 50 mediocre workers
- Better to reject bad workers than let businesses have bad experiences

### 4. Speed is Feature
- Businesses want workers NOW
- Worker yang bisa cepat respond = lebih banyak jobs

### 5. Feedback Loops
- Workers improve dengan ratings
- Businesses improve dengan reviews
- Platform improves dengan data
